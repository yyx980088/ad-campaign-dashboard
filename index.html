Metric definitions

What each figure on this dashboard means and how it's calculated.

Impressions
The total number of times an ad was displayed to users, regardless of whether it was clicked.
Ad Clicks
The number of times users clicked on an ad after it was shown to them.
CTR (Click-Through Rate)
Ad Clicks ÷ Impressions × 100. Measures how compelling an ad is at converting views into clicks.
CPC (Cost Per Click)
Total Cost ÷ Ad Clicks. The average amount spent each time a user clicks on an ad.
Total Cost
The full amount spent running a campaign over the given period, including media and platform fees.
Generated Revenue
The total revenue attributed to a campaign — sales or conversions traced back to ad clicks.
ROI (Return on Investment)
(Revenue − Total Cost) ÷ Total Cost × 100. The percentage return earned on every dollar of ad spend.
Demographic Segment
The audience group (gender and age band) that most engaged with a given campaign run.
